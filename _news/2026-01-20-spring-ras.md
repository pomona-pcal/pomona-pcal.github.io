Henry Cannon, Connor Wang, and Stevie Kim have all joined the PCAL group working on individual projects as RAs. Henry is working on a secure/compressed co-designed memory, Connor is working on a parallelized version of the gem5 simulator, and Stevie is working on a concurrent skip list for CXL.
